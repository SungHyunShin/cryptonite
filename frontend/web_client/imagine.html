Imagine

Just imagine: what if you knew Bitcoin would be as valuable as it is today, 6 years ago? Or, what if you didn't sell your Monero before it crashed? With this tool, you can see the net results of buying a cryptocurrency days, weeks, or even years ago.

To use this feature, type a number of days into past you'd like to invest in the 'days' field. Then, choose one of the current top 15 cryptocurrencies from our dropdown menu. Finally, set an amount of that crypto you would have invested in all those years ago. Click the + button if you'd like to add another cryptocurrency to invest in.
